Who am I?

I am a young freelance software developer and electronic music producer from America. For the last nine years of my life, I have dedicated my life to learning the ins and outs of software development, and have tinkered with everything from front-end and back-end web development to game development. I have also spent the last year teaching myself how to produce electronic music, and it has been a wild adventure.

Music and computers are my two main passions, but I also am an experienced graphic designer, photographer, and content producer. I also love to work out, stay fit, and push my body to its limits. I see every day as a new opportunity to learn and better myself so I can better help those around me.

What do I know?

I've done a little bit of everything, from JavaScript to Java, HTML to Ruby. I have experience in over a dozen languages, as well as numerous popular libraries and frameworks, such as Vue for front-end web development, Express for back-end web development, Unity for game development, JavaFX for desktop application development, and discord.js for Discord bot development to name a few.

Most recently, I have been experimenting with Python, Crystal, C++, and Go. Although I have experience with most of these already, I do not know as much as I would like. I am also trying to get better at CSS, as it is a challenge to make user-friendly web experiences that are simultaneously beautiful.

Outside of specific languages and libraries, I have experience with SQL, managing and planning for large projects, and working directly with clients.
